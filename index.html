Grand Seiko: the nature of time

Grand Seiko has a long tradition of drawing inspiration from its surroundings in Japan, from the ripples of Lake Suwa to the snow on Mount Iwate. The natural world plays a defining role in its designs – notably with the textured dial of the White Birch watch, which references the forests near the brand's studio in Shizukuishi.
